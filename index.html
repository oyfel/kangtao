// 01 — DOSSIER

The Company

Kangtao Arms is a vertically integrated weapons and security conglomerate, designing everything from sidearms to autonomous turret networks under one production roof. What began as a regional foundry contract now ships hardware to corporate security divisions, private military contractors, and federal procurement programs across three continents.

Every product line is built on the same operating principle: reliability under contested conditions. Kangtao engineers don't chase the flashiest spec sheet — they chase the gun that still cycles when the power's out, the rain hasn't stopped, and the client's life depends on the next trigger pull.

The company operates two primary brands under its corporate umbrella: Kangtao, the flagship line for tactical and combat hardware, and QianT, its precision and civilian-adjacent subsidiary focused on sidearms and concealable systems.
